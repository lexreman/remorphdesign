 1: All printed parts can fit on 250x250 plate. 
 
 2: Real ASA-CF or better, such as PPS-CF is recommended. 
 
 3: Some parts need considerably more perimeters and infill percentage. 
 
 4: Some parts have been designed with infill as filters. 
 
 5: Some parts require excellent short bridges capabilities. 
 
 6: Very few parts require support. 
 
 7: For great aesthetics, check cad assembly for part orientation, 
    and paint seam at unlikely seen position once installed.
